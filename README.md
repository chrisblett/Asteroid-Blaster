## How to Play
Destroy asteroids and enemy ships to earn score and obtain crystals. Collect 10 crystals to win

## Controls
*	UP    – Thrust
*	LEFT  – Turn left
*	RIGHT – Turn right
*	SPACE – Fire
*	SHIFT – Activate shield
